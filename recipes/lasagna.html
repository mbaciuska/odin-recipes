Lasagna Recipe (Brenda's)

Description: This beautiful lasagna is actually something I can never eat because I unfortunately have a dairy allergy. This is a test website that I am creating in an attempt at completing TOP.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) jar spaghetti sauce
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • ½ pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • ½ pound shredded Cheddar cheese
  • pint ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Grab frozen lasagna from grocery store
  2. Pre-heat oven to 425
  3. Bake frozen lasagna for 25 minutes
  4. Devour and enjoy. Yum!
